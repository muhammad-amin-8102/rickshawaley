About Us

  • We connect you to Auto-rickshaws from your locality, particularly for long rides. That's it! That's all we do.
  • We don't intervene in your arrangement with them, and we don't charge anything, neither from you, nor from them for the rides booked.
  • Just choose the city and the area you're in, and call up the rickshaw walas listed there. They'll reach in no time as they know that you too belong mostly to the same area and that you're booking a long ride.
